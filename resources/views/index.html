About me

My name is Fabian, music addict, drummer, coder, currently living in Hamburg. Music has been a passion for since being six years old, dancing to the records of my parents (Pink Floyd, Wishbone Ash, Canned Heat...) The obscure, the psychedelic, the raw is still something that can be found in my nowadays music taste, ranging from folk, heavy metal to silence set to music.
